# Companion Planting Reference

## Compatibility Matrix

| Plant       | Good With                        | Bad With             | Notes                              |
|-------------|----------------------------------|----------------------|------------------------------------|
| Tomato      | Basil, Pepper, Carrot, Marigold  | Fennel, Brassicas    | Basil repels aphids/hornworms      |
| Pepper      | Tomato, Basil, Green Onion, Garlic | Fennel, Kohlrabi   | Onions/garlic deter aphids/beetles |
| Basil       | Tomato, Pepper                   | Sage                 | Improves tomato flavor; deters whiteflies |
| Cucumber    | Nasturtium, Dill, Beans, Peas, Radish | Sage, Potato    | Nasturtiums trap aphids/cucumber beetles |
| Zucchini    | Nasturtium, Marigold, Dill       | Potato               | Needs 3–4' spread; check for vine borers |
| Kale        | Arugula, Nasturtium              | Strawberry           | Both brassicas — share pests       |
| Arugula     | Kale, Nasturtium                 | —                    | Bolts quickly in heat (85°F+)      |
| Marigold    | Most vegetables                  | —                    | French varieties for nematode suppression |
| Nasturtium  | Cucumber, Zucchini, Tomato       | —                    | Trap crop for aphids; let spill outward |
| Dill        | Cucumber, Zucchini               | Tomato (mature)      | Attracts parasitic wasps           |
| Green Onion | Pepper, Tomato                   | Beans, Peas          | Mild aphid/beetle deterrent        |
| Garlic      | Pepper, Tomato                   | Beans, Peas          | Mild pest deterrent; needs full sun |

## Pest-Control Plant Placement (South Louisiana)

| Pest Target          | Plant           | Strategy                          |
|----------------------|-----------------|-----------------------------------|
| Aphids               | Nasturtiums     | Trap crop near cucumbers/squash   |
| Hornworms            | Basil, Dill     | Basil deters; dill attracts parasitic wasps |
| Whiteflies           | Basil           | Between tomatoes                  |
| Cucumber beetles     | Nasturtiums     | Near cucumbers                    |
| Nematodes (soil)     | French Marigolds| Along bed edges                   |
| Squash vine borers   | —               | No effective companion; wrap stems with foil/netting |
| Cabbage loopers      | —               | Affects kale/arugula; row cover helps |
