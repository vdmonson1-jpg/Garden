## Soil Structure Notes

- **Issue observed**: Standing water on surface; hydrophobic topsoil.
- **Corrective plan**: Fork-aerate + add pine bark fines or expanded shale (10–20% by volume, top 6–8").
- **Important for wildflowers**: Do not over-amend. Wildflowers bloom better in moderately lean, well-drained soil.
- Skip blood meal and bone meal for this bed. ½" Black Kow max if soil seems depleted.
- Priority is drainage correction, not fertility.
