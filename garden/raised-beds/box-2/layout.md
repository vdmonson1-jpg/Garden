## Placement Rationale

- Tomatoes centered for vertical training.
- Basil tucked between tomatoes for pest deterrence.
- Peppers at far end past trellis footprint — full unobstructed sun.
- Green onions and garlic near peppers (shallow roots, mild pest deterrent).
- Marigolds along outer walkway edge.
- Existing fall flowers left in place — seasonal transition crop.
