## Key

| Symbol     | Plant              | Spacing              |
|------------|--------------------|----------------------|
| Cucumber   | Cucumber           | 18–24" apart         |
| Zucchini   | Zucchini           | Last 2–3' of bed     |
| Arugula    | Arugula            | 6–8" clusters        |
| Kale       | Kale               | 12–15" from stems    |
| Nasturtium | Nasturtiums        | Spill outward        |
| Dill       | Dill               | Toward end           |
| Marigold   | French Marigolds   | Along outer edge     |

## Placement Rationale

- Cucumbers centered on trellis line, offset 6–8" from base.
- Kale and arugula near trellis for partial shade benefit — transitional spring crops.
- Zucchini at far end with full 2–3' of dedicated space; will spill outward.
- Nasturtiums along outer edge as trap crop.
- Dill near end to attract beneficial insects.
- No garlic on the zucchini end — leaves will smother it.
