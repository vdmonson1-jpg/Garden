## Orientation Notes

- All beds run **east–west**.
- South-facing sides receive strongest, most direct sun.
- North-facing sides get slightly less intense exposure.
- Arched trellis connects Box 3 and Box 4 (cucumbers climb from Box 3 side).
- Sun intensity is high (South Louisiana). Tomatoes tolerate full south exposure; cucumbers benefit from slightly softer light on the north side.
- Walkways surround all beds on all sides.
- All beds are **18" deep, open to native ground** (crumbly, slightly sandy subsoil — good drainage below raised bed layer).
- **Drainage concern**: topsoil has become hydrophobic from past over-amendment. Water pools on surface but soil stays dry below. Corrective plan: fork-aerate + add pine bark fines for structure.
